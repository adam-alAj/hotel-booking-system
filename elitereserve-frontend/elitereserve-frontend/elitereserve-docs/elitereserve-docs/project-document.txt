2. PROJECT DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

SIMPLE IDEA:
This system is an online application, that allows people to book in hotels via the
internet.
The user creates an account on the system, and can choose hotel(global hotels), Technology
choose a suitable roomtype, with of course deciding the check in and checkout Database
in advance, when he chooses the room type in a hotel, he can book and pay. This system
makes it easier for people as a hotel guide, and booking for a guarentee.


SYSTEM USAGE FLOW:
[1] Login page(register if no account yet, gets email messege for new account)
[2] Main home page with hotels (pagination used), the user also has to determine the check in and checkout date, and number of people
[3] Selected hotel shows available room types with prices per night
[4] User selects aroom type and book (the system automatically assingns a room for him with that room type)(gets message of the booking)
[5] User get payment window,and has to pay for the price according to the available pricing rule applied (get notified via email)
[6] Done! the user booked via the system (can also cancel or extend the booking)


HOW EACH FUNCTIONAL SERVICE WORKS (IN THE MONOLITH, LETS SAY THE PACKAGES IN THE PROJECT):

[1] ADMIN: this is made for admin extra control over session review and management.

[2] CATALOG: which contains hotels, room types and rooms. A customer is supposed to see all the hotel, chooses a hotel
             then he views the room types available, so he can book a room type (a 
             room is implicilty booked for the user by the system, as when he books he also gets to know
             his room number in the hotel when he arrives in the hotel at check in date).
             And when it comes to HOTEL_MANAGER, he can view and manage data of each hotel, that he manages. So he can 
             create add rooms, room types to the hotel.

[3] BOOKING: when a customer finds his perfect hotel and room type, he can book. When he books he has to pay, via the PAYMENT
             service, and the price calculated by the PRICING service. The flow goes like this: the customer finds his best room types, and 
             creates a booking, so the booking state is now pending, when he pays for the booking ID, the booking is now
             confirmed. After he books he can of course extend the booking (delay the checkout date), and he can also cancel the booking 
             within 48 hours with a 66% refund of money. Of course all booking process are tracked with email notifications
             sent from the NOTIFICATION service.

[4] PAYMENT: the role of the payment service is only used by the customer, as he has to pay for the booking, in case he makes a new
             booking, or has to pay the difference in price if he extends. So the payment posted always holds 2 IDs, one for the main booking, and 
             one for the extension. If booking is made is initial, the extensionId=null. If he extends, he sends the ID of the booking he wants to extend
             plus the ID of the extended booking (which is the same booking actually, has different calculations for paying).

[5] FEEDBACK: this service is made for the system to keep in track what the users(customers) think about the service. A customer sends his feedback directly
              to the feedback entity in the databse (via of course the feedback service).

[6] PRICING: this service is used to calculate the final price of the staying, using the hotel pricing rules (HOTEL PRICING RULE SUB SERVICE), and the           
             the (HOTEL BASE PRICE SUB SERVICE), with taking in notice the base prices of the room types. The flow works like this: when a customer chooses his hotel,
              room type, and dates, a calculation is made (pricing rules are applicable on dates and days included in the booking period + taking in notice the bas prices 
              of the hotel + and the room types standard base price). At the end of the calculation the customer gets a final quotedPrice to pay (which is the base price, with made calculations
              could be extra money or a discount, depending on the pricing rules applicable on every day in the period reserved).

[7] RATING: this service allows customers to rate the hotel and the staying after the booking is ended, which helps the system admin tracking
            the quality of hotels he has hosted in his system + plus giving to the next customers the average rating of each hotel, which
            help them pick the best quality for the best money.

[8] NOTIFICATION: this is the notifications service, that notifys users via email service, when the register on elitereserve, and when the created a booking, pay successfully for
                  a booking, or requests an extension and pays for it, and also when a booking he made is cancelled or expired out of date.

[9] USER: which are the users of the system, whether they are customers (CUSTOMER), hotel managers (HOTEL_MANAGER), or 
          admins (ADMIN). and they are almost associated with all entities in the system,as without a user there is no functionality made.

[10] AUTHENTICATION: this service is called the auth service, and it is used to athenticate users when the log in, via JWTs (BEARER type) and ROLE defrentiation.
                     When a user logs in (he can register if he has no account existed on the system),he gets an access token, and a refresh token (High Security).
                     The system uses his refresh token he gets, to refresh the access token, as access tokens don't last for ever.    
                                                   

KEY FEATURES:
[1] User registration & authentication
[2] Hotel search & filtering
[3] Online booking & reservations
[4] Payment integration
[5] Room management(for hotel)
[6] Booking management/cancellation/extending
[7] Email notifications
[8] Review & rating system

POLICIES:
[1] The booking can pend the payment for 15 minutes, if not payed, the booking is expired. Must book again
[2] Customer gets refunded 2/3 of the amount only during 48 hours after the book, after 48 he can't cancel (no refund)
[3] The payment process for the booking can only happen three times, after three times the booking is not valid (has to book again)
[4] EliteReserve is not responsible for a mistake between the hotels and customers, it is only a guide for cudtomers, and a broker for hotels.
[5] EliteReserve shows the rating of each hotel to the customer for best advice
[6] A hotel manager can manage many hotels
[7] A big resposibility for the hotels is that room managing must be manually tracked, such that rooms under maitainance or booked by cash, must be set not available


TARGET USERS: 
 - Customer: reviews hotels, room types, and books.
 - Hotel Manager: can manage and review his hotel data.
 - System Admin: can manage and review data transferred via the system, and can do incase issue solving if occured

4. ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Architecture Pattern: MONOLITHIC 

ARCHITECTURE FLOW:
==> DB -> Repository -> Service -> Controller -> Client (and vise versa).

*** Data is transferred via DTOs (Data Transfer Objects).
*** For each service there is a mapper, that converts from DTO (create/update) request to entity, and fron entity to DTO (response).
